Why Bowers & Wilkins?

B&W discovered a very lightweight carbon dome allowing them to extend its usable frequency response out to more than double the range of human hearing to an incredible 47,000 Hz.
Bowers & Wilkins leverages over 50 years of experience in building some of the finest high performance loudspeakers in the world.
B&W has a rich pedigree in speaker design, and its 800 series is the pinnacle of the British company’s impressive lineup.
